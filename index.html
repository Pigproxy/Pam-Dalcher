Chapter I

A Life Between the Pages

We are deeply saddened to share the passing of Pam Dalcher, who dedicated her life to fostering a love of reading as a grade school librarian. Pam had a gift for putting exactly the right book in a child's hands at exactly the right moment.

Her warm smile made the school library feel like the most magical room in the building. She remembered every child's reading level, every favorite character, and every story that made their eyes go wide.

Her School

Pam served the students and families of L.E. Starke School in Pekin, Illinois for many dedicated years.

📚 L.E. Starke School — Pekin, IL

A fundraiser has been established in Pam's memory so that children in need can purchase books at future book fairs. Your donation lets a child walk up to a table full of books and pick one — any one — that is entirely their own.

Pam believed every child deserved the gift of a story to call their own. Your generosity helps keep that belief alive.

The Goal

Every dollar donated goes directly to L.E. Starke School's book fair fund — no fees, no overhead.
